Galadriel Validation Engineer

Validation engineers manage, inspect, calibrate, test and modify the instrumentation, equipment, mechanics and procedures used in manufacturing. A critical aspect of their job is documenting this process so there is evidence that systems are operating safely and efficiently.

Personality Traits of validation Engineer

  1. Consults with management, assesses customer requirements, and studies product characteristics to select validation objectives and standards.
  2. Prepares protocols for validation or performance testing of new or modified manufacturing equipment, processes, or systems.
  3. Analyzes data from validation tests to determine whether systems and processes meet required criteria and specifications.

    4. Physical Traits of validation Engineer

  1. Prolonged periods sitting at a desk and working on a computer.
  2. Must be able to lift up to 15 pounds at times.
  3. Must be able to manipulate equipment for calibration and modification.
